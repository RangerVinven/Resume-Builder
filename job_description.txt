Full job description

FT Recruitment is working with our forward-thinking and dynamic business that is looking to further grow its development team. This position joins a collaborative, technical and high-performing development team. As a Senior Developer, the successful candidate will play a key role in designing, developing, and maintaining high-quality applications and solutions across our clients' technology stack. The successful candidate will leverage their expertise in SQL, C#, .NET, Python, Azure, and Microsoft’s Power Platform. You will also work with Microsoft Fabric to deliver modern data-driven solutions.

Key Responsibilities

    Design, develop, and maintain high-quality applications using C#, .NET, and Python
    Create reliable data solutions by writing and optimising SQL queries, views, and stored procedures
    Build, enhance, and support applications with Power Apps, Power Automate, and the wider Power Platform
    Deliver modern analytics and reporting capabilities using Microsoft Fabric
    Design and implement cloud-based solutions on Azure, ensuring they are scalable, secure, and high-performing
    Work closely with business analysts, product owners, and stakeholders to turn requirements into effective technical solutions
    Lead code reviews, mentor junior developers, and promote best practices and coding standards
    Support system integrations, resolve performance issues, and maintain high system availability
    Keep up to date with emerging technologies and recommend innovative solutions

The successful candidate will have a development background with extensive exposure to C#, .NET, SQL and Python. Any experience of using Power Platform is beneficial but not essential. You must be fluent in German for this job.
